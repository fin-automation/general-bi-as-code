## # ClaimEase AI - Presentation Deck README

## Overview

**ClaimEase AI** is a 7-slide presentation deck designed for Deriv, showcasing an AI-powered employee reimbursement system that simplifies and accelerates the claims process.

---

## Deck Structure

| Slide | Title | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | Title Slide | Introduction to ClaimEase AI brand and concept |
| 2 | Introducing ClaimEase AI | Key features and value proposition |
| 3 | Current State: The Challenge | Problem statement for employees and business |
| 4 | Complete Visibility | Employee dashboard and balance tracking |
| 5 | The Journey | Step-by-step claim submission flow |
| 6 | 24/7 Personal Benefits Expert | AI assistant capabilities |
| 7 | Efficiency Gains That Matter | ROI metrics and key results |

---

## Key Metrics Highlighted

- **50%** faster claim submission (10 min reduced to 5 min)
- **90%** fewer errors via AI validation
- **15+ hours** weekly time savings across the organization

---

## Design Specifications

- **Primary Color:** Red/Coral `#F44336` (Deriv brand)
- **Accent Color:** Teal Green `#14B8A6`
- **Background:** White / light pink tint
- **Typography:** Bold sans-serif headers, clean body text
- **Style:** Modern, minimal, visual-heavy

Deriv internal stakeholders, HR leadership, and product teams evaluating AI-powered benefits management.
